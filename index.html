Our story

A family counter that grew into a neighbourhood

Wamumbi Supermarket opened its doors on Kimathi Street in 2011 as a single fruit-and-vegetable stall run by the Wamumbi family. Word travelled on the quality of the produce alone, and within a few years the stall had grown into a full grocery store.

Today we stock eight full aisles — groceries, fresh produce, dairy, bakery, beverages, snacks, household and personal care — but we've kept the habits that got us here: checking every crate before it hits the shelf, and greeting regulars by name.

"We don't just sell groceries — we restock the same kitchens, week after week. That's a responsibility we take seriously."

— The Wamumbi family
